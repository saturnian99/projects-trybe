Banana Shake

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 2 un
Strawberry 150 g
Milk 250 ml
Whey/
Powdered milk		 30 g

Step 1: Put everything on a blender and shake it up.
Step 2: Enjoy this delicious and healthy shake!

Morning Omelet

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3 un
Tomato 1/2 un
Mozzarella Cheese 2 slices

Step 1: Mix eggs and tomatoes on a bowl.
Step 2: Add salt and spice it up.
Step 3: Fry the contents on a frying pan.
Step 4: When the omelet is ready, place the cheese slices on top of it. Then fold it.
Step 5: Congratulate yourself! Eggs are so versatile...
